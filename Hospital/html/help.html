To search for a patient, navigate to the Patients section from the sidebar. You can search by:

  • Patient ID — e.g. SH-00412
  • Full Name — type part of the name
  • Admission Date — filter by date range

Results update in real-time as you type. Click a patient row to open their full profile.

Tip: Use the "Advanced Filter" button for multi-criteria search.

Medical reports can be generated from the Reports section or directly from a patient profile:

  • Open the patient's record
  • Click "Generate Report" in the top bar
  • Review the AI analysis and vitals charts
  • Optionally add Clinical Notes via the modal
  • Click "Export PDF" to download

Reports include ECG waveform, vital trend charts, AI findings, and timeline.

Tip: Clinical Notes section only appears in the PDF if you fill it in.

Safe Heart AI continuously monitors patient vitals and triggers alerts based on thresholds:

  • Critical — Immediate intervention required (e.g. SpO₂ < 90%, HR > 140 BPM)
  • Warning — Parameters trending toward concern
  • Stable — Vitals within acceptable range

Alerts appear in the Alerts panel, in-app notifications, and optionally via email/SMS (configurable in Settings).

Tip: Enable "Auto Alerts" in Settings > System Preferences for AI-driven predictive alerts.

Access Settings from the sidebar (bottom section). Available options include:

  • Profile Settings — name, email, hospital, photo
  • System Preferences — dark mode, notifications, language
  • Security — change password with strength indicator
  • Notification Settings — critical, email, SMS toggles

All changes take effect immediately after clicking Save.

For platform emergencies or urgent technical issues:

  • Emergency Hotline: +20 100 86 — 24/7
  • Support Email: support@safeheart.com
  • Live Chat: Available Mon–Fri, 08:00–22:00

For patient medical emergencies, contact the on-call cardiologist directly through the hospital PABX system.

For life-threatening emergencies, call the local emergency number immediately.
